Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 10th May, 2025

This Privacy Policy outlines how ToDo Daily ("we", "our", or "us") handles user information and data. We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our app is designed to function entirely offline, ensuring that your data remains on your device and is not transmitted elsewhere.

1. Information Collection
We do not collect any personal information. All tasks and reminders you create are stored locally on your device.

2. Permissions
To provide core functionalities, our app requests the following permissions:

  • Exact Alarms: Allows the app to schedule precise reminders for your tasks.
  • Notifications: Enables the app to send you reminders about your tasks.

These permissions are solely used to enhance your experience within the app. We do not access or collect any data through these permissions.

3. Data Storage
All your data, including tasks and reminders, are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store any of your data on external servers.

4. Data Sharing
We do not share your data with any third parties. Your information remains solely on your device.

5. Data Security
While we prioritize your privacy, please be aware that the security of your data also depends on your device's security measures. We recommend keeping your device updated and secured.

6. Data Retention and Deletion
Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over its retention and deletion. Uninstalling the app will remove all stored data.

7. Children's Privacy
Our app is accessible to users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect, use, or disclose personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the app.

9. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at ghexoft@gmail.com
